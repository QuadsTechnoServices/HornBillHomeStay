Thrilling Activities

Canopy Walk

India’s First Canopy walk in the Western Ghats installed at a height of 30feet above the ground near Castle Rock. The 240 meter walkway will provide a bird eye view of the Verdant undulating landscape. Tourists can visit by paying additional cost set by the forest department.

Doodhsagar Waterfalls

The world renowned “Doodhsagar waterfall“ is just 5km from Hornbill Homestay. We provide you the opportunity to visit the topmost point from where it originates. The point offers an unforgettable view of Sahyadri Mountains range, clad in dense green forest.

Wildlife Photography

Everyone love photography today but nothing comes close to heart except wildlife photography. Hornbill Homestay gives you the opportunity to click the best photos of different birds and wild animals.

Nature Walk

A walk in nature begins as a serenity by allowing our overstimulated modern minds a chance to rest. It helps us to relax, it lowers our stress, depression and anxiety levels. It increases our attention spans and memory and it provides a source of gentle exercise.

Vajra Falls

After construction of Kali project, Vajra falls remain submerged from June to November. After November tourists can visit Vajra Falls.

Western Ghats

Often called the Great Escarpment of India, the Western Ghats are a chain of hills, running parallel to the West Indian Coast. One among the eight hottest biodiversity hotspots, the region is recognized as UNESCO World Heritage site.

Trekking

Surrounded by thick forest and mountains, Hornbill Homestay provides you with the option to trek and explore. The topmost part of renowned “Doodhsagar Waterfall” is just 5km from Homestay and the trekking route goes from Hornbill Homestay.

Bonfire

Bonfire has its own charm. It just feels so peaceful siting by the fire and sipping your favorite dish.
