1. How to reach 'HORNBILL HOME STAY'...

    1. By Road.

    - It is on Belgaum-Panjim national highway NH 4-A.

    a) From Belgaum:

    - It is 85km from Belgaum. To come to Castle-Rock, you need to take the Belgaum-Panjim NH-4-A Highway and from Ramnagar after around 25km (1 KM short of village Anmod), you need to take a semi-graveled road to your left for Castle-Rock. After travelling 8km on this semi-graveled road through jungle, you will reach Castle-Rock. “ Whistling Thrust Home Stay” is further inside 5KM Diggi road.

    b) From Panjim:

    - It is 85Km from Panjim. Take Belgaum-Panjim NH-4 –A Highway and after climbing the picturesque Anmod ghat (one KM; from Anmod village), turn to your right for Castle-Rock.

    c) From Hubli/ Dharwad/ Bangaluru: It is 80km from Dharwad. To come to Catle-Rock, travel on Dharwad-Goa Road via Ramnagar to reach Castle-Rock.

    2. By Air

    - From Mumbai guests can come by air to Dabolim Airport at Goa or Hubli Airport & then take taxi to Castle-Rock.

    3. By railway.

    a) If you are coming from Mumbai by Konkan Railway then one should ideally take ‘Jan-Shatabdi’ up to Madgaon and then board ‘Goa Express’ (Vasco-Nizamuddin Express) which leaves Madgaon at 3.30pm & reaches Castkle Rock at 6pm. Transport can be arranged for pick-up, if prior intimation is received.

    b) If you are coming from Mumbai you can also take Chalukya Express (From Dadar) and reach Londa by 11 am and then take a taxi to Castle-Rock.

    c) If you are coming from Pune you can take either Chalukya Express (to reach Londa) or Ekakulam Express (to reach Caslte-Rock), Train reaches at 10am.

    d) From Bangalore take Kittur-Chennamma Express to reach Londa.
